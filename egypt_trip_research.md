# Egypt Trip Research Notes

## Dubai (Layover - 9 hours)
### City Introduction
- Modern metropolis in UAE
- Mix of ultramodern architecture and traditional souks
- Key attractions accessible within half-day tour

### Suggested Half-Day Itinerary for 9-hour Layover
1. **Burj Khalifa** - World's tallest building, iconic skyline
2. **Dubai Mall & Dubai Fountain** - Shopping and fountain shows
3. **Burj Al Arab** - Iconic sail-shaped hotel (photo stop)
4. **Jumeirah Beach** - Open beach views
5. **Gold Souk & Spice Souk** - Traditional markets
6. **Dubai Marina** - Modern waterfront district

## Cairo
### City Introduction
- Capital of Egypt, "City of a Thousand Minarets"
- Founded in 969 by Fatimids
- Cultural capital of Middle East for centuries
- Coexistence of Pharaonic, Greco-Roman, Coptic, and Islamic civilizations
- UNESCO World Heritage Site (Historic Cairo)

### Attractions
1. **Pyramids of Giza** - Ancient wonders, iconic Egyptian monuments
2. **Grand Egyptian Museum (GEM)** 
   - World's largest museum dedicated to one civilization
   - 490,000 square meters
   - 100,000 ancient artifacts spanning 7,000 years
   - Includes Tutankhamun Galleries, Khufu's Boats Museum
   - Located 2km from Giza Pyramid Complex
3. **Khan el-Khalili (哈里里市场)** - Historic souk, maze of streets and stalls
4. **Saladin Citadel** - Medieval fortress built by Sultan Saladin, panoramic views, alabaster mosque
5. **Cave Church (St. Simon Monastery)** - Unique church carved into Mokattam Hills

### Hotel
**Hilton Cairo Nile Maadi** - On the Nile River

## Aswan
### City Introduction
- Southern Egyptian city on the Nile
- Known for tranquil beauty and Nubian culture
- Gateway to ancient temples

### Attractions
1. **Philae Temple** - Island temple dedicated to goddess Isis
2. **Aswan High Dam** - Modern engineering marvel
3. **Old Cataract Hotel** - Historic British colonial hotel (1899), luxury afternoon tea venue, featured in Agatha Christie's "Death on the Nile"
4. **Nile Sunset Cruise (Felucca)** - Traditional Egyptian sailboat, tranquil waters at dusk

### Hotel
**Pyramisa Island Hotel Aswan (Isis Island)** 
- Exotic resort on island in middle of Nile
- 28 acres of landscaped gardens
- Magnificent Nile views
- Near center of Aswan

## Luxor
### City Introduction
- Ancient Thebes, religious capital of Ancient Egypt
- Home to world's largest religious building (Karnak)
- East and West Bank attractions

### Attractions
1. **Karnak Temple Complex** 
   - Largest religious building ever made
   - Covers 200 acres (1.5km x 0.8km)
   - Place of pilgrimage for nearly 2,000 years
2. **Luxor Temple** - Large ancient temple complex on east bank
3. **Valley of the Kings** - Royal tombs of pharaohs
4. **Colossi of Memnon** - Two massive stone statues of Pharaoh Amenhotep III (erected 1350 BC)
5. **Medinet Habu (Temple of Ramesses III)** - Magnificent memorial temple with colorful battle scenes

### Hotel
**Steigenberger Resort Achti**
- 5-star resort on east bank of Nile
- 281 guest rooms
- Stunning views of river and Theban hills
- Southern end of Luxor

### Route: Aswan to Luxor
- 4 hours drive
- Scenery: Nile River views, desert landscapes, agricultural areas along the Nile

## Hurghada
### City Introduction
- Red Sea resort city
- Famous for pristine beaches, coral reefs, marine life
- Water sports paradise

### Attractions & Activities
1. **Dolphin House** - Swim with wild dolphins in natural habitat
2. **Snorkeling** - Vibrant coral reefs, colorful marine life
3. **Desert Safari** - Quad biking, dune bashing
4. **Beach Relaxation** - All-inclusive resort experience

### Hotel
**Hilton Hurghada Plaza**
- Beachfront on private Red Sea beach
- 3km from downtown
- Three seafood restaurants
- Two poolside bars with sea views
- All-inclusive resort

### En Route Attraction
**Dendera Temple Complex**
- Temple of Hathor
- One of best-preserved temple complexes in Egypt
- 2.5km southeast of Dendera, close to Luxor
- Beautiful entrance with six massive columns with head of Hathor
- Includes basilica, two birth houses, sacred lake

### Route: Luxor to Hurghada (via Dendera)
- Approximately 4-5 hours total
- Stop at Dendera Temple
- Scenery: Desert landscapes, Red Sea coastal views

### Route: Hurghada to Cairo
- 5 hours drive
- Scenery: Red Sea coastal road, Eastern Desert, beautiful desert vistas
